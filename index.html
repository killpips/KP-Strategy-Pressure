1. Introduction

The Killpips KVO (Killpips Volume Overlaps) indicator, released in Beta version, has been developed to apply the Killpips strategy in a structured manner through advanced volume analysis in relation to specific proprietary levels called KVO.

The indicator's objective is to provide a clear reading of market directionality and high-probability operational areas, using a proprietary algorithm that combines price dynamics, volume pressure, and reference levels.

2. Foundations of the Strategy

Core Elements

  • KVO Levels: Killpips Volume Overlaps
  • Volume Pressure: Dynamics and intensity
  • Context: Signal contextualization in the market

Analysis Rule

Signals generated by the indicator must never be interpreted in isolation, but always analyzed in relation to KVO levels and the current market structure.

3. KVO Levels

KVO Daily levels represent areas of significant volume concentration and overlap.

Characteristics

  • Automatically calculated and plotted
  • Derive from proprietary volumetric algorithms
  • Fixed and non-modifiable inputs
  • Act as dynamic market support and resistance

The algorithm uses KVO levels as the primary reference for interpreting volume pressure and generating operational signals.

4. Signal Types

Momentum Signals

Indicate probability of continuation.

  • Generated when volume supports price
  • Effective near KVO levels or breakouts
  • Highlight active market participation

Contrarian Signals

Indicate potential exhaustion.

  • Emerge with volume imbalances/absorption
  • Manifest near KVO levels
  • Anticipate reversals or corrections

Overlapping Signals

Both Momentum and Contrarian on the same candle.

  • Coexistence of directional pressure
  • Simultaneous exhaustion signals
  • Action: Strengthen contextual analysis

5. Confirmation & Timing

  • All signals are confirmed exclusively at candle close.
  • The opening of the next candle represents the technical reference for operational entry.

This approach reduces unconfirmed intrabar signals and promotes discipline.

6. Volume Fill & Pressure

Colored Fill

Graphically displays volume pressure.

  • Color varies by volume intensity
  • Assess movement continuity
  • Support for position management

Broken Line

Signals reduced volume pressure.

  • Volumes losing intensity
  • Signs of slowdown/reversal
  • Warning: Protect profits

7. Input Structure

ALGO Parameters

  • Factor: (1=Scalping, 2=Directional, 5=Swing)
  • Primary Momentum: Regulates intensity
  • Secondary Momentum: Influences calculation degree
  • Contrarian: Defines sensitivity

Trading Session

  • Select markets of interest
  • Establish active time windows

KVO Daily Levels

  • Preset and non-modifiable
  • No manual adjustment required

9. Operational Management

Stop Loss

  • Above/below signal candle
  • Beyond nearest KVO level (Recommended)

Targets

  • Next KVO level
  • Manual trailing using colored fill
